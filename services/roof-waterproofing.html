Protect Your Home from Water Damage

A leaking roof can cause serious damage to your home, including wood rot, mold growth, and damage to your ceilings and walls. Our professional roof waterproofing services can provide a durable, long-lasting barrier against water intrusion, protecting your home and giving you peace of mind.

Our Waterproofing Services Are Essential For:

  • Flat roofs
  • Balconies and patios
  • Parapet walls
  • Chimneys and flashings
  • Areas prone to water pooling

Our Roof Waterproofing Process

  1. 1. Thorough Inspection

    We'll conduct a comprehensive inspection of your roof to identify any existing leaks or areas of potential water intrusion.

  2. 2. Surface Preparation

    We'll prepare the surface of your roof by cleaning it and making any necessary repairs to ensure a smooth, sound substrate for the waterproofing membrane.

  3. 3. Primer Application

    We'll apply a high-quality primer to the roof surface to ensure proper adhesion of the waterproofing membrane.

  4. 4. Application of Waterproofing Membrane

    We'll apply a durable, seamless waterproofing membrane to your roof, paying special attention to vulnerable areas such as seams, flashings, and penetrations.

  5. 5. Final Inspection and Water Testing

    We'll conduct a final inspection of the waterproofed area and perform a water test to ensure that your roof is completely watertight.
