Restore Your Roof's Beauty and Integrity

Over time, roofs can accumulate dirt, moss, algae, and lichen, which can not only make your roof look old and dirty but can also cause damage to the roofing materials. Our professional roof cleaning services can safely and effectively remove these contaminants, restoring your roof's appearance and extending its lifespan.

Our Roof Cleaning Services Are Ideal For:

  • Removing dirt, grime, and pollution
  • Eliminating moss, algae, and lichen growth
  • Improving the appearance of your home
  • Preventing damage to your roofing materials
  • Preparing your roof for painting or other treatments

Our Roof Cleaning Process

  1. 1. Inspection and Assessment

    We'll inspect your roof to determine the extent of the cleaning required and identify any areas that need special attention.

  2. 2. Gentle Pre-Treatment

    We apply a specially formulated, eco-friendly cleaning solution to your roof to loosen dirt and kill moss, algae, and lichen.

  3. 3. Low-Pressure or High-Pressure Wash

    Depending on your roof type, we'll use either a gentle low-pressure wash or a high-pressure wash to safely and effectively clean your roof.

  4. 4. Post-Treatment and Protective Coating

    After cleaning, we can apply a protective coating to your roof to help prevent future growth of moss, algae, and lichen.

  5. 5. Final Rinse and Clean-up

    We'll give your roof a final rinse and clean up the surrounding area, leaving your property looking clean and tidy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my roof?

We recommend professional roof cleaning every 2-3 years, depending on your location and environmental conditions. Regular cleaning prevents damage and extends roof life.

Is high-pressure cleaning safe for all roofs?

We adjust our cleaning methods based on your roof type. Some materials require gentle cleaning, while others can withstand high-pressure cleaning.

How long does roof cleaning take?

Most residential roof cleaning projects are completed within a day. The exact duration depends on roof size and condition.

Are your cleaning products safe?

Yes, we use eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for your roof, plants, and the environment while effectively removing dirt and growth.
