Transform Your Roof with a Fresh Coat of Paint

A professional roof painting service can dramatically improve the appearance of your home, protect your roof from the elements, and increase its lifespan. At RoofMasters, we use only the highest quality, weather-resistant paints and coatings to ensure a long-lasting, beautiful finish.

Our Roof Painting Services Include:

  • Thorough roof inspection and cleaning
  • Minor repairs to tiles and flashing
  • Application of a high-quality primer
  • Two coats of premium roof paint in the color of your choice
  • A 5-year workmanship guarantee

Our Roof Painting Process

  1. 1. Inspection and Quote

    We start with a thorough inspection of your roof to assess its condition and provide you with a detailed, no-obligation quote.

  2. 2. Preparation

    We prepare your roof for painting by pressure cleaning it to remove all dirt, debris, and loose paint. We also perform any necessary repairs to ensure a smooth, even surface.

  3. 3. Priming

    We apply a high-quality primer to your roof to ensure proper adhesion of the paint and to provide an extra layer of protection.

  4. 4. Painting

    We apply two coats of premium, weather-resistant roof paint in the color of your choice. Our experienced painters use professional equipment to ensure a flawless finish.

  5. 5. Clean-up and Final Inspection

    We clean up the work area and conduct a final inspection of your roof to ensure that you are completely satisfied with the results.
